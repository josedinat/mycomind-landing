The Solution

Meet My-CoMind, Your cognitive continuity system

My-CoMind captures the moments where your thinking happens. Decisions. Conversations. Insights. Trade-offs. It connects them into a living knowledge graph of your reasoning. So when you ask: “Why did we choose this?” You don’t get a note. You get the context behind the decision.
